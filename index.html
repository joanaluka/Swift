How it works

Mobile Money Payments,utility customers load money on their prepaid water meters using Mobile Money such as MPesa, with any phone, at any time, and for any amount.

What we do

Consumers can track usage, load credit remotely, and decrease the possibility of bill shock due to leakages or incorrect monitoring. A prepaid water meter can also be used to limit water flowing to a particular area.

Our Product

Prepaid Water Meter locks once units are depleted and opens when correct token is entered. Prepay Water Meter locks if tampered with.

About us

SwiftPay Meter is a distributor of a wide variety of high quality prepaid water meters. We have prepaid water meters for communal water points, for domestic connections and ATMs. All prepaid water meters are linked to mobile payment and come with an online monitoring system for tracking daily payments and water consumption. Online monitoring also helps to detect leakages and illegal connections and greatly reduces non-revenue water.
